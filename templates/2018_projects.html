JPMorgan Chase & Co: Code For Good Hackathon

October 2018

This is a 24 hours Hackathon in Chicago, Illinois. I collaborated with a group of 6 students to help a Non-Profit organization called Chinese Mutual Aid Association by creating a Web Application to help solve their parent translation and student enrollment problem.

We also helped visual student progress and provide extra homework practice for students.

I became Lead Front-End Developer to create the Front-End of the web application.

This was my first hackathon and we received 2nd place winner out of 17 competing teams. Our project will continue to be used in JP Morgan Chase's Force for Good Hackathon
